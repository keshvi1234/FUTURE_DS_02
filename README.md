Overview:
This task focuses on analyzing customer churn and retention patterns using the Telco Customer Churn dataset.
The main objective of this dashboard is to identify why customers leave the service and understand the factors that influence customer retention.
The dashboard was created using Power BI to transform raw customer data into meaningful business insights.

Objectives:
Analyze customer churn behavior
Identify high-risk customer groups
Understand the impact of contract type, internet service, payment method, and tenure on churn
Visualize retention trends and customer patterns
Generate business insights for improving customer retention

Tools Used:
Power BI
Power Query
DAX Measures
CSV Dataset

Dashboard Features:
The dashboard includes
Total Customers
Churn Customers
Retention Rate
Churn by Gender
Churn by Contract Type
Churn by Internet Service
Churn by Payment Method
Monthly Charges Distribution
Churn Trend by Tenure
Senior Citizen Churn Analysis
Interactive Filters

Key Insights:
The overall retention rate is 73.46%
Month-to-month customers show the highest churn rate
Fiber optic internet users are more likely to churn
Customers using electronic check payment method have higher churn tendency
Customers with shorter tenure are more likely to leave
Churn distribution between male and female customers is almost equal
